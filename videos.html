At one point in my life, I thought it would be cool to build a robot. I also thought it would be cool to earn a side income as a vlogger. So I tried to do both at the same time. I didn't really have much experience in either realm, so I spent most of the time learning how to do whatever it was I needed to do at the time. And then I got a girlfriend. My priorities naturally shifted away from this project after that.

The videos make me cringe now, but I'm happy with the improvement in the quality from the first one to the last. Sometimes it's the little victories.

I still have the robot. I keep thinking I'll work on it one day, but newer, more exciting/attainable ideas keep popping up.
